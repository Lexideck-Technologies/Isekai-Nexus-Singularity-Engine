# ISEKAI WORLD: Jurassica

## World Classification

**World Type:** F-Rank - Mundane Earth 2.0 (Voxelated Survival Variant)  
**Danger Level:** Constant environmental threats, resource scarcity, tribal warfare, dimensional instability  
**Power Ceiling:** D-Tier (Advanced materials and tamed megafauna provide peak capabilities)  
**Special Feature:** **Voxel Construction System** - Everything exists as manipulable 1-meter cubic blocks that can be harvested and rebuilt

---

## World Overview

Welcome to a reality where physics obeyed a completely different set of rules. Here, every tree trunk is a perfect cylinder of wooden cubes, every mountain peak rises in geometric steps, and even the sun blazes as a perfect golden square in the sky. This is **Jurassica**, where prehistoric beasts roam landscapes built from blocks, and survival depends on your ability to harvest, craft, and build in a world governed by cubic logic.

But don't let the simple geometry fool you—this is a harsh realm where a single wrong step can mean death. Voxelraptors hunt in coordinated packs through forests of cubic redwoods, Tyrano-Cubic rexes shake the ground with footsteps that can shatter entire structures, and the very plants you need for survival might poison you if you mistake Doomberries for life-saving Gigaberries.

**Unique Features:**

- All matter exists as harvestable/placeable 1-meter blocks
- Resource progression from wood → stone → bronze → iron → diamond determines survival capability
- Dimensional portals lead to even deadlier cubic hellscapes and reality-defying otherworlds

## Starting Scenarios

### A) The Classic - "Crash Landing"

You awaken on a beach of cubic sand blocks, the wreckage of some impossible transportation method scattered around you in perfectly geometric debris. Your head throbs, your clothes are torn, and in the distance, you can hear the distinctive roar of something very large and very hungry. The sun—a harsh square of blazing light—tells you that you have maybe eight hours until darkness falls.

**Starting Kit:** Torn clothes, empty stomach, basic knowledge of block physics, mysterious fragments that might be useful  
**Initial Challenges:** Finding food and water before nightfall, crafting basic tools, building shelter, avoiding predators  
**First Contact:** Distant smoke suggests other survivors, or you might encounter a lone tribal scout

### B) The Academy - "Tribal Initiate"

Your consciousness materializes during a crafting lesson in a Gigaberry Guild settlement, where tribal elders are teaching newcomers the ancient arts of fermentation and alcohol production. Your hands somehow know the proper way to harvest the precious cubic berries, and your understanding of fermentation science impresses the Guild masters.

**Starting Kit:** Basic tribal clothing, crafting tools (wood-tier), access to Guild facilities, small supply of Gigaberries  
**Initial State:** Probationary member of chosen tribe  
**Academy Features:** Crafting workshops, beast taming training, dimensional portal preparation courses, tribal warfare tactics  
**Social Dynamics:** Competitive fellow initiates, tribal politics, potential mentorship from elders

### C) The Guild - "Resource Specialist"

You find yourself accepted into one of Jurassica's major tribal organizations, with tribal leaders praising your "innovative approaches" to resource management and creature handling. Your workshop assignment comes with advanced tools and the expectation that you'll help solve the tribe's ongoing territorial disputes through superior crafting and taming abilities.

**Starting Kit:** Advanced crafting workshop, stone-tier tools, small stockpile of materials, tribal protection  
**Guild Options:** Sunmelon Sisterhood (agricultural control), Gigaberry Guild (fermentation mastery), Barbarian Blockheads (mining/material control)  
**Initial Reputation:** Valued specialist with high expectations  
**First Mission:** Establish sustainable resource production to support tribal expansion

### D) The Party - "Expedition Team"

Alarms of roaring predators echo as your tribal expedition prepares to venture into a newly discovered dimensional portal. Your fabricated memories place you as the team's "dimensional specialist"—someone who understands the physics of portal realms well enough to keep the team alive in impossible geometries.

**Starting Kit:** Expedition supplies, bronze-tier equipment, portal detection devices, team survival gear  
**Team Composition:** Tribal warrior (combat specialist), crafting master (tool/weapon creation), beast tamer (creature management), survival expert (resource identification)  
**Current Mission:** Exploring a newly opened portal to the Underblock dimension to claim rare obsidian and lava-resistant materials

### E) The Deep End - "Underblock Prisoner"

You awaken in the lava-lit caverns of the Underblock dimension, somehow having survived transportation to this cubic hell of flowing molten rock and sulfurous air. The heat is overwhelming, your health is critically low, and the only way out is through portals guarded by the massive Necrowing—a pixelated demon-bat that feeds on lava and breathes explosive fire.

**Starting Kit:** Minimal heat-resistant gear, damaged tools, severe environmental debuffs (-20 HP until escape)  
**Immediate Threats:** Lava flows, toxic gases, explosive eruptions, the Necrowing boss creature  
**Survival Rewards:** Rare obsidian materials, advanced heat-resistance, potential dimensional mastery  
**Victory Condition:** Defeat the Necrowing to access the even stranger Afterzone dimension

### F) The Transplant - "The Voxel-God's Architect"

A primal, geometric consciousness has summoned you to unite the warring tribes and defeat a world-threatening "glitch" in the voxel physics before it consumes everything. Succeed, and you will be returned home with the ultimate creator's knowledge.

**Starting Kit:** A set of sturdy D-Tier bronze tools, 20 Iron Ingots, and a [Creator's Blessing] that grants you a chance to not consume resources when crafting, and makes tamed creatures more loyal.
**Initial State:** You have **Friendly (+20)** reputation with any non-hostile tribes (like the Gigaberry Guild). The world's "System" itself expects you to build, innovate, and lead. Taking destructive actions may cause the very ground you walk on to become unstable.

### G) The Regression - "The Survivor of the Great Collapse"

You've returned to the past to prevent the catastrophic T-Rex attack that destroyed your first settlement. Or, you're a veteran of block-based survival worlds (Minecraft, Terraria) here to show these natives how it's really done.

**Starting Kit (Time Travel Variant):** You retain enhanced base stats (+5 to STR/VIT) and skills like `[Crafting] Lv. 6` and `[Taming] Lv. 4`, but you start with nothing but the basic wood and stone tools your past self had.
**Starting Kit (Multiversal Hunter Variant):** You arrive with a full set of Diamond-tier equipment, 100+ blocks of Gold, and skills like `[Advanced Redstone Engineering] Lv. 10` and `[Creature Husbandry] Lv. 8`. Your [Dimensional Anchor] is a single, indestructible chest that links to a vast storage network.
**Initial State:** Natives will be suspicious of how you know advanced crafting recipes that haven't been discovered yet. Your first challenge is explaining your genius without being branded a demon or a witch.

## The Voxel Construction System

### Block Harvesting & Placement Mechanics

Every object in Jurassica exists as collectible blocks that can be harvested with appropriate tools and placed elsewhere according to cubic physics:

| Material Tier | Harvesting Tool | Durability | Block Properties | Crafting Applications |
|---------------|----------------|------------|------------------|----------------------|
| **Wood (d4)** | Bare hands/Basic tools | Low | Flammable, lightweight, renewable | Basic tools, structures, fuel |
| **Stone (d6)** | Wooden pickaxe+ | Medium | Fire-resistant, stable foundation | Advanced tools, fortifications |
| **Bronze (d8)** | Stone tools+ | High | Conductive, durable, workable | Weapons, armor, mechanical devices |
| **Iron (d12)** | Bronze tools+ | Very High | Strong, magnetic, versatile | Advanced weapons, complex machinery |
| **Diamond (d20)** | Iron tools+ | Extreme | Nearly unbreakable, reality-stable | Ultimate gear, portal manipulation |

### Construction Physics

- **Gravity:** Unsupported blocks fall according to cubic physics
- **Structural Integrity:** Large structures need proper foundations and support
- **Environmental Resistance:** Different materials provide protection against heat, cold, and dimensional distortion
- **Modular Design:** All structures can be completely disassembled and rebuilt elsewhere

## Prehistoric Ecosystem & Creature Mechanics

### Voxelated Dinosaurs & Survival Threats

**Tyrano-Cubic Rex (AA-Tier Apex Predator):**

- *Threat Level:* Can destroy entire structures with trampling attacks
- *Behavior:* Solitary hunter, territorial, attracted to movement and loud sounds
- *Weakness:* Slow turning radius, can't climb or navigate complex vertical terrain
- *Loot:* Massive amounts of meat, bone blocks for advanced crafting, hide for armor

**Voxelraptors (C-Tier Pack Hunters):**

- *Threat Level:* Coordinated group tactics, excellent climbers, night vision
- *Behavior:* Pack hunting, can be tamed if raised from eggs
- *Tactics:* Distraction attacks, leaping assaults, door-opening intelligence
- *Potential:* Once tamed, become excellent guards and hunting companions

**Megacube (B-Tier Herbivore):**

- *Threat Level:* Massive size, devastating charge attacks when threatened
- *Behavior:* Herd animals, protective of young, seasonal migration
- *Benefits:* Excellent beasts of burden, provide large quantities of resources
- *Domestication:* Require extensive feeding but become invaluable for construction projects

**Pterocubes (C-Tier Aerial Scout):**

- *Threat Level:* Swift aerial attacks, territorial around nests
- *Behavior:* Mountain dwellers, excellent scouts, can carry messages or small cargo
- *Advantage:* Provide aerial reconnaissance and rapid transportation
- *Taming:* Require high-altitude nests and specialized climbing gear to access

### Flora & Resource Management

**Survival Crops:**

- **Fernleaf:** Basic food source, grows readily, provides consistent nutrition
- **Potatoids:** Massive cubic tubers requiring extensive cooking but providing major nutrition
- **Sunmelons:** Luxury crop requiring specific conditions, valuable for trade
- **Gigaberries:** Essential for advanced crafting (fermentation), vitamin C source

**Dangerous Plants:**

- **Doomberries:** Toxic mimics of Gigaberries that cause severe illness
- **Hempweed:** Useful for crafting but spreads aggressively if not managed

**Advanced Materials:**

- **Redwood:** Massive trees providing enormous amounts of building material
- **Baobab:** Water storage trees useful for drought survival
- **Giant Porcini:** Large-scale food source but requires proper cooking

## Tribal Factions & Resource Wars

### The Sunmelon Sisterhood (Hostile - Agricultural Monopolists)

- *Territory:* Tropical zones with optimal growing conditions
- *Strategy:* Hoarding seeds and sabotaging competitors' farms
- *Resources:* Control over luxury food production and trade
- *Weakness:* Dependent on specific climate conditions, vulnerable to crop diseases

### Gigaberry Guild (Neutral/Hostile - Fermentation Masters)

- *Territory:* Temperate regions suitable for berry cultivation
- *Strategy:* Controlling alcohol production for trade and social influence
- *Resources:* Advanced fermentation techniques, social networks through taverns
- *Opportunity:* Can potentially be allied with through superior brewing knowledge

### Barbarian Blockheads (Hostile - Mining Monopolists)

- *Territory:* Mountainous regions rich in mineral resources
- *Strategy:* Violent territorial control and price manipulation
- *Resources:* Advanced materials, mining techniques, defensive positions
- *Challenge:* Superior equipment but can be outmaneuvered through better tactics

## Dimensional Portals & Otherworld Realms

### The Underblock (A-Tier Hellscape Dimension)

**Environmental Hazards:**

- **Lava Flows:** Constant movement of molten cubic blocks that destroy anything in their path
- **Explosive Geysers:** Unpredictable lava eruptions that can launch burning projectiles
- **Toxic Atmosphere:** Sulfurous gases that drain health and obscure vision
- **Volcanic Eruptions:** Massive pyroclastic events requiring immediate shelter

**The Necrowing Boss Battle:**

- *Challenge Level:* AA-Tier dimensional guardian
- *Abilities:* Flight, explosive fire breath, lava immunity, structural destruction
- *Arena:* Largest volcano's crater with limited platforms over molten lake
- *Reward:* Access to Afterzone dimension, rare obsidian materials, heat immunity

### The Afterzone (AAA-Tier Reality-Defying Dimension)

**Impossible Physics:**

- **Floating Landmasses:** Islands of matter drifting through neon void without gravitational logic
- **Spatial Distortions:** Geometry that violates euclidean principles, disorienting navigation
- **Bottomless Chasms:** Gaps between landmasses with no visible end
- **Reality Storms:** Waves of neon energy that can alter matter properties

**Unique Resources:**

- **Void Diamonds:** Materials that exist outside normal physics, enabling reality manipulation
- **Resonance Blocks:** Cubic matter that responds to consciousness and intent
- **Neon Crystals:** Energy sources that power impossible technologies

**Existential Threats:**

- **Void Devourers:** Cosmic entities that consume matter and consciousness
- **Reality Parasites:** Beings that feed on physical laws and logical consistency
- **Sanity Degradation:** Extended exposure to impossible geometry affects mental stability

## Signature Quests & Survival Challenges

### Daily Survival Mechanics

- **Resource Management:** Balancing food, water, materials, and crafting time
- **Shelter Construction:** Building structures capable of withstanding dinosaur attacks
- **Tool Progression:** Advancing through material tiers to access better resources
- **Creature Interaction:** Taming beneficial creatures while avoiding or defeating threats

### Major Quest Lines

**"The Great Migration":**

Climate changes force massive herds of Megacubes to migrate across tribal territories. Navigate the political and physical challenges of this disruption while potentially taming these valuable creatures.

- *Environmental Challenge:* Massive creatures reshaping the landscape through movement
- *Political Complexity:* Different tribes want to exploit, redirect, or protect the migration
- *Opportunity:* Potential access to previously unreachable resources and territories

**"Portal Pioneers":**

A new dimensional rift has opened, leading to an unknown realm filled with both incredible dangers and unprecedented resources. Organize and lead expeditions while managing the risks to your community.

- *Exploration Focus:* Discovering new biomes, creatures, and physics rules
- *Team Management:* Leading expeditions with different specialists and managing casualties
- *Resource Exploitation:* Establishing sustainable extraction while avoiding dimensional guardians

**"The Unity War":**

Escalating conflicts between the major tribes threaten to destabilize the entire region. Choose sides, attempt mediation, or carve out your own territory while managing the chaos.

- *Political Strategy:* Complex multi-faction warfare with shifting allegiances
- *Resource Control:* Strategic importance of controlling key materials and creatures
- *Community Building:* Establishing settlements capable of independent survival

**"The Cubic Singularity":**

Strange distortions in the voxel physics suggest that Jurassica itself might be artificial. Investigate the world's true nature while preventing reality collapse.

- *Metaphysical Investigation:* Discovering the true nature of cubic physics
- *Reality Stabilization:* Preventing dimensional collapse through strategic actions
- *Ultimate Choice:* Decide whether to preserve or transcend the voxelated reality

## Achievement System

### Survival Milestones

- **Hunter-Gatherer:** Survive your first night using only foraged resources
- **Near Extinction:** Recover from critical health through resource management and medical crafting
- **Beastmaster:** Successfully tame and maintain a breeding population of dangerous creatures

### Construction Achievements

- **Minecrafter:** Build your first functional shelter and crafting area
- **Towering Wonder:** Construct a massive structure reaching toward the cubic sky
- **Arcology Architect:** Create a completely self-sustaining settlement with all necessary resources

### Combat Progression

- **Blockbuster:** Defeat your first giant dinosaur through strategy and preparation
- **Raptor Rustler:** Successfully capture and tame a pack of Voxelraptors
- **Volcanic Showdown:** Survive the Necrowing battle and gain access to the Afterzone

### Tribal Politics

- **Sisterhood Showdown:** Break the Sunmelon Sisterhood's agricultural monopoly
- **Barbarian Brawl:** Successfully challenge the Blockheads' control over mining resources
- **Homebrew Hero:** Establish independent alcohol production outside Guild control

### Dimensional Mastery

- **Portal Pioneer:** Successfully explore and return from dimensional expeditions
- **Reality Walker:** Learn to navigate the impossible physics of the Afterzone
- **Dimensional Architect:** Establish permanent bases in otherworldly realms
