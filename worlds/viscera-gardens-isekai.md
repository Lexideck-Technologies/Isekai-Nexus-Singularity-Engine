# ISEKAI WORLD: THE VISCERA GARDENS

## World Classification // (Revised for v4.2)

**World Type:** [A Rank] - [Biopunk / Hyper-Evolutionary / Living Tech]
**Danger Level:** [Extreme] - The environment is actively predatory; the air carries mutagenic spores, and civilization is built upon the bones of the conquered.
**Power Ceiling:** [AA Rank] - Biological Immortality, City-Level Hive Minds, and Planetary Terraforming via Spore Clouds.

## World Overview

The Viscera Gardens is a world where the Industrial Revolution never happened; instead, the **Biological Revolution** consumed everything. Metal is rare and regarded as "dead matter," while plastic is unknown. Architecture is grown from calcified bone-coral and hollowed-out Ironwood trees. Transportation relies on domesticated, colossal millipedes, and energy is harvested from the bio-luminescent nervous systems of captive leviathans.

Humanity survives here not by conquering nature, but by rewriting their own genetic code to withstand it. This is the era of **"The Splice,"** where your social standing is determined by the quality of your grafts. The wealthy flaunt designer wings and filtered lungs, while the poor suffer from "gene-rot" and unstable mutations.

The central conflict rages between the **Sages of the Helix**, who pursue a post-human godhood through forced evolution, and the **Terra-Naturalists**, insurgents who believe the "Human Form" is sacred and must be preserved against the encroaching flesh.

**Climate/Environment:** The atmosphere is dense, oxygen-rich (35%), and humid. It creates a greenhouse effect where flora grows at visible speeds. The "seasons" are dictated by the migration patterns of airborne spore-clouds (The Pollen Winter vs. The Fungal Summer).

## Starting Scenarios

### A) The Classic - "The Tabula Rasa"

[Survival scenario - Awakening in the "Feral Zone"]

**Starting Kit:**

* **Chitin-Knife (F-Tier):** A sharpened serrated limb from a giant insect.
* **Spider-Silk Loincloth:** Durable but offers zero protection against toxins.
* **[Dormant Symbiote Seed]:** Implanted in the chest. Requires distinct biomass to awaken.

**Initial Challenges:** The local flora is carnivorous. You must identify safe water sources (most water contains parasitic larvae) and hunt vermin to feed the Symbiote before it begins to metabolize your own organs for sustenance.
**First Contact:** **Grak**, a tribal scavenger with chameleon-skin grafts who mistakes your lack of mutations for a rare "Ancestor Spirit" omen.

### B) The Academy - "The Helix Institute"

[Educational institution scenario - The Oxford of Genetic Horror]

**Starting Kit:**

* **White-Coat Bio-Armor:** Resistant to acid and blood stains.
* **Portable Gene-Sequencer:** Allows analysis of slain monsters.
* **Department Allowance:** 500 *Cells* (Currency).

**Initial State:** [Freshman Researcher]. You are looked down upon for having "Default DNA."
**Academy Features:**

* *The Vivisection Hall:* Combat training involves dissecting living beasts.
* *The Greenhouse:* Forbidden zone containing plants that scream.
**Social Dynamics:** The **Flora Track** (Plant Mages) vs. The **Fauna Track** (Beast Shifters). You must pick a major or risk being used as a test subject.

### C) The Guild - "The Harvesters"

[Professional organization scenario - Monster hunting for parts]

**Starting Kit:**

* **Needle-Rifle (D-Tier):** Fires compressed air and bone shards.
* **Preservation Vials:** To keep organs fresh.
* **Harvest Map:** Markings of known nests.

**Guild Options:**

* **The Reapers:** High-volume pest control.
* **The Extractors:** Specialized retrieval of glands, eyes, and rare fluid sacks.
**Initial Reputation:** [Meat-Shield].
**First Missions:** "The Kidney Run." Locate a **Mimic-Tiger** and extract its filtration organ without rupturing the toxin sacs.

### D) The Party - "The Chimera Squad"

[Pre-existing group scenario - A tactical mutant response team]

**Starting Kit:** [Bio-Graft: Night Vision] and [Bio-Graft: Adrenaline Gland].
**Party Composition:**

* **Torque (Tank):** Has Rhinoceros Beetle plating grafted to his torso and arms.
* **Flutter (Scout):** Hollow bones and dragonfly wings; incredibly fragile but fast.
* **Bloom (Healer):** A plant-symbiote host who secretes healing nectar and paralytic pollen.
**Initial Mission:** Penetrate a **"Dead Lab"** (an abandoned facility) to retrieve a unstable "Omega Strain" sample before the Sages do.

### E) The Deep End - "The Compost Pit"

[High-risk scenario - Awakening in a disposal site]

**Starting Kit:** None. You are naked and wounded.
**Immediate Threats:** **The Rejects**—failed experiments that are piles of weeping flesh, teeth, and misguided limbs. The environment itself is highly acidic.
**Survival Rewards:** Unique Title: **[Survivor of the Filth]**. Grants high resistance to toxins and disease + unlocks the **"Scavenger Genetics"** tree (can eat rotting food safely).

### F) The Transplant - "The Prototype"

[High-powered start - Created/Summoned in a lab]

**Starting Kit:** Talent **[Omni-Gene]**. You can assimilate *any* DNA without the risk of "Rejection Sickness."
**Recruiter:** **Grand Sage Ozymandias**.
**Purpose:** You are "Project Adam." You are designed to hunt down the **Apex-Leviathans**, consume their essence, and become the god-king that unifies the ecosystem.

### G) The Regression - "The Apex Predator"

[Veteran returning to a Level 1 body]

**Regression Path (Awakening):** You were the "King of the Jungle" in a previous timeline.
**Starting Kit:** **[Mental Map of the Genome]**. You know exactly which monsters yield the best mutations.
**Impending Disaster:** The **"Devolution Plague"** is three months away. It turns sapient humans into feral beasts. You must evolve immunity before it hits.

## Geography & Major Locations

### The Central Cortex (Civilization)

* **Corporea, The Living City:** The capital. The buildings are grown from massive, hollowed-out biological constructs. The city "breathes," and the streets are lined with bioluminescent veins.
* **The Spire of Life:** A tree reaching the stratosphere, housing the Sages.

### The Wilds

* **The Hyper-Veldt:** A grassland where the grass is razor-sharp and the grazers are armored tanks.
* **The Bile Fens:** A swamp of digestive acids. Home to the Hydra-Boas.
* **The Canopy Kingdoms:** A vertical civilization living in the treetops, safe from ground-based predators but vulnerable to aerial horrors.

## Power Systems & Magic

### Primary System: The Splice (Bio-Augmentation)

Magic does not exist here. Powers are biological functions gained through surgery or mutation.

**Casting Method:** **Biological Trigger.** (e.g., Flexing a specific muscle to fire a bone spur, or accelerating heart rate to activate adrenaline).
**Mana Source:** **Biomass / Calories.** Using abilities burns hunger. Overuse leads to starvation and death.
**Skill Requirements:** **[Fortitude]** (to survive the surgery) and **[Synchronization]** (to control the new limb).

**Key Mechanic: Genetic Instability**
If a player grafts too many conflicting creature parts (e.g., Reptile scales + Mammal fur + Insect wings), their **Instability** rises. At 100%, they succumb to **"The Feral Change"** and become an NPC monster.

### Secondary System: Pheromones (Control)

**Source:** Glands extracted from Queen-type monsters.
**Usage:** Influencing NPCs, taming beasts, or inducing fear. Requires [Alchemy] to synthesize.

## Isekai Integration Elements

### Modern Knowledge Applications

* **Virology & Sanitation:** The world knows nothing of germ theory, only "bad humors" and "curses." Simple sterilization prevents gangrene, a major killer here.
* **Genetics (CRISPR):** Understanding DNA structures allows the user to optimize grafts better than the local "guess-and-check" surgeons.
* **Ballistics:** Gunpowder is a lost art. Reintroducing explosives disrupts the biological armor meta.

### Cheat Skill Synergies

* **[Appraisal]:** Acts as a high-tech DNA scanner. Allows the user to see exactly what "buffs" a monster organ will give before eating/grafting it.
* **[Inventory]:** Prevents organic matter from decaying. The user can store fresh organs indefinitely, creating a massive economy advantage.

### Romance & Relationship Opportunities

Romance in the Viscera Gardens is complex. "Beauty" is subjective (scars and useful mutations are attractive). Relationships often involve **"Blood Pacts"** or sharing genetic material to create stronger offspring.

* *Potential Interest:* **Princess Alara**, daughter of a Sage Lord. She wants to escape her father's experiments. She is beautiful but has lethal, retractable spines.

* **"The Cull":** Daily quests to thin out rapidly breeding pest populations. Rewards: Biomass.
* **"Sample Retrieval":** Fetch quests for specific glands. Rewards: Currency (Cells).

### Major Quest Lines

**"The Genome Project"**
The player must hunt down the "Seven Prime Beasts" and consume/graft their essence.

* *Rewards:* Unlocks the **[Perfect Organism]** form.

**" The Cure or The Catalyst"**
A plague is sweeping the slums. The Sages want to accelerate it to weed out the weak; the Naturalists want a cure.

* *Stakes:* The fate of the capital city. Choosing a side permanently locks out the other faction.

## Unique Viscera Gardens Features

### The Hunger Mechanic

Using skills burns calories rapidly.

* **Gameplay Impact:** You cannot just "rest" to regain mana. You must eat. This forces a predatory playstyle. Combat -> Eat Enemy -> Evolve.

### The Evolution Tree

Instead of a Skill Tree, players have a **Mutation Tree**.

* **Body Slots:** Head, Torso, Arms, Legs, Skin, Internal.
* **Feature:** You can swap parts. Need to swim? Graft gills. Need to fight? Graft armor.

### Achievement System

### Culinary Arts

* **[Iron Stomach]:** Eat raw meat without getting sick 50 times.
* **[Gourmet]:** Consume the flesh of 10 different Boss monsters.

### Evolution Milestones

* **[Genetic Abomination]:** Reach 90% Instability without turning feral.
* **[Pure Blood]:** Reach Level 50 without a single graft (Hard Mode).

### Ultimate Achievements

* **[Apex Predator]:** Defeat Ozymandias and Gorgon. Sit atop the food chain.

***"In the Gardens, you do not adapt to the world. You force the world to adapt to you. Change, or become compost."***
