# ISEKAI WORLD: The Chroma Clash

## World Classification

**World Type:** AA-Rank - Conceptual Universe/World of Gods and Demons Hybrid  
**Danger Level:** Reality-warping color conflicts, temporal distortions, dimensional instability, spectrum warfare  
**Power Ceiling:** S-Tier (The Prism itself exists outside normal time and space, Chromatic Royals who transcend mortality, reality-rewriting light magic)

---

## World Overview

The Chroma Clash is a surreal science fantasy realm where colored light is the fundamental force of existence itself. Here, beings of pure spectral energy—the magnificent Chromatics aligned to Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Indigo, and Violet—wage eternal war for control of The Prism, a mysterious crystal structure that produces reality-warping beams of multicolored light.

Geography defies logic in this dreamlike world where landmarks shift hues like living paintings and terrain features take impossible forms. Time flows differently under various colored lights: red, orange, and yellow regions experience accelerated time where centuries pass in moments, while blue and violet areas slow temporal flow to near-stillness. Travel occurs through unstable passages where intersecting light beams fold space itself, allowing instant transit across vast distances—but journey through colorless darkness may last eternities.

The Chromatics themselves are beings of living light who can transform into pure energy beams, traveling at lightspeed while experiencing reality through their spectral nature. Against them stand the enigmatic Monochromats—colorless beings who lack light powers but possess the unique ability to analyze, anchor, and manipulate the abilities of others. Between these factions, reality itself becomes the battlefield as colored light rewrites the fundamental laws of existence.

**Environment:** Surreal landscapes painted in impossible hues, temporal distortion zones, dimensional passages of intersecting light, ruins trapped in time bubbles.

## Starting Scenarios

### A) The Classic - "Prismatic Awakening"

Your consciousness emerges in a kaleidoscopic wilderness where rainbow light fragments dance through the air. As you observe your reflection in a crystal pool, you discover you've somehow become attuned to one of the seven colors—or perhaps you're something even more unique. The nearest settlement glows with a single dominant hue, but do its inhabitants welcome strangers?

**Starting Kit:** Clothes matching your spectral attunement (F-Tier), basic light-focusing crystal  
**Initial Challenges:** Understanding color-based reality, avoiding hostile spectrum territories, discovering your true nature  
**First Contact:** Monochromat guides, refugees from spectrum warfare, or welcoming same-color communities

### B) The Academy - "Spectrum Scholar Exchange"

You materialize within the Prismatic Academy, a neutral zone where all seven colors study the mysteries of light and reality. Registered as an "interdimensional research fellow," you have access to advanced chronomantic techniques and spectrum manipulation training—though some colors eye you with suspicion.

**Starting Kit:** Academy robes (shifting all colors) (D-Tier), spectrum analysis tools, library access credentials, chrono-stabilizer device  
**Initial State:** Neutral reputation with all Chromatic factions  
**Academy Features:** Light magic theory, temporal mechanics, dimensional geography, Prism studies  
**Social Dynamics:** Competing color representatives, ancient Monochromat professors, mysterious hybrid students

### C) The Guild - "Spectrum Services Registry"

You find yourself in the Rainbow Bazaar, a swirling marketplace where Monochromat traders facilitate exchanges between hostile color factions. Your credentials show certification as a "neutral specialist" offering services across spectrum territories—a dangerous but profitable profession.

**Starting Kit:** Neutral gray attire (D-Tier), choice of spectrum tools/temporal anchor/reality scanner, trader's license, 500 prismatic credits  
**Guild Options:** Chrono-Navigators (temporal travel), Light Brokers (spectrum diplomacy), Reality Engineers (dimensional construction), Prism Seekers (artifact recovery)  
**Initial Reputation:** Neutral with all factions  
**First Missions:** Deliver messages across hostile territories, guide time-lost travelers, broker peace talks

### D) The Party - "Spectrum Research Team"

You awaken as the newest member of a multi-color research expedition investigating temporal anomalies near The Prism itself. Your fabricated memories suggest joining as a specialist in an area matching your abilities. The team represents a rare alliance between traditionally hostile colors for a greater purpose.

**Starting Kit:** Expedition gear (C-Tier), spectrum communication device, established team relationships  
**Party Composition:** Red warrior-scientist, Blue temporal theorist, Green nature-harmonist, Monochromat coordinator  
**Initial Mission:** Investigate reality distortions that threaten to unravel the fundamental structure of colored light

### E) The Deep End - "Chromatic Prisoner"

Consciousness returns in a crystalline cell where hostile Chromatics hold you captive, believing you're a spy from an enemy spectrum. Your escape means navigating territory where the very air burns with light that could unmake your existence, while reality shifts unpredictably with each color's influence.

**Starting Kit:** Prisoner restraints (must be removed), severe light burn injuries (-10 HP until healed)  
**Immediate Threats:** Hostile spectrum guards, reality distortion zones, temporal traps  
**Survival Rewards:** Potential Spectrum Survivor title, chrono-resistance abilities, rare light crystals

### F) The Transplant - "The Prism's Emissary"

The sentient Prism itself, desperate to prevent its own corruption, has summoned you. Your heroic destiny is to unite the seven warring Chromatic Courts against the rising threat of the Null King. Succeed, and you will be returned to Earth, your mind imbued with a perfect understanding of light and reality.

**Starting Kit:** A modest but complete set of neutral-gray robes (D-Tier) that can attune to any color, a focus crystal, and a unique [Prismatic Blessing] that allows you to cross between color territories without harm and marks you as a holy figure to all Chromatics.
**Initial State:** You have **Friendly (+20)** reputation with all seven Chromatic Courts, a fact that makes each of them deeply suspicious of the others' influence over you. You are a figure of hope and a catalyst for political paranoia.

### G) The Regression - "The Veteran of the Color War"

You have returned to a time before the Great Betrayal, when one of the Chromatic Royals attempted to seize the Prism, shattering the old alliances and starting the eternal war. Or, you are a multiversal traveler who has seen realities made of sound, mathematics, and pure emotion, and this world of light is just another puzzle to solve.

**Starting Kit (Time Travel Variant):** You retain enhanced conceptual stats (+5 to INT/VIT) and skills like `[Color Weaving] Lv. 6` and `[Temporal Mechanics] Lv. 4`, but you are a simple, unattuned Monochromat with no standing.
**Starting Kit (Multiversal Hunter Variant):** You arrive with a suit of prismatic armor that can channel all seven colors (B-Tier), a collection of reality-stabilizing artifacts, and skills like `[Conceptual Warfare] Lv. 9` and `[Multiversal Diplomacy] Lv. 8`. Your [Dimensional Anchor] is a pocket dimension of pure white light from which you can draw power.
**Initial State:** The Chromatic Royals of the past will sense your "future-tainted" light. Your challenge is preventing a war they believe is inevitable without being erased as a temporal anomaly.

- *Location:* Mobile hideouts that shift between different color territories

- *Location:* Roams areas of extreme temporal distortion

- *Motivation:* Mindless hunger for temporal energy, growing stronger with each chronological meal
- *Weakness:* Can be trapped in stable temporal loops created by coordinated spectrum effort

**The False Prism - Reality Mimic (S-Tier):**

- *Location:* Creates deceptive copies of The Prism to lure victims
- *Power:* Near-perfect reality manipulation, can create false worlds and identities
- *Motivation:* Replace the true Prism with itself to control all colored light
