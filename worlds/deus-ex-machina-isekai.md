# ISEKAI WORLD: DEUS-EX-MACHINA

## World Classification

**World Type:** [Rank SSS] - The Celestial Gear (Conceptual Universe / Mecha Divinity)
**Danger Level:** Apocalyptic / Theological
**Power Ceiling:** Infinite (Literal Godhood)

## World Overview

This is not a planet; it is a **Dyson Sphere** built around a captive White Dwarf star. The surface area is billions of times larger than Earth. The "mortals" (trillions of beings from thousands of species) live on the outer shell, a high-tech landscape of cathedral-cities and sacred factories.

Above them, in the vacuum of the "Heavens," reside the **Divinities**. These are bio-mechanical entities of unfathomable scale—**Seraph-Class Mecha** standing 7,000 kilometers tall. Their halos are orbital defense rings; their wings are vast solar-sail arrays that gather starlight. They do not speak; they **Broadcast**. The entire radio spectrum is filled with the "Voice of God"—a cacophony of digital prayers, commands, and static.

When the Divinities war, the sky burns. A "thunderstorm" is actually the shockwave of a Titan-Sword cleaving the atmosphere. A "shooting star" is a drop of coolant leaking from a wounded god, large enough to drown a continent.

**Climate/Environment:** The weather is dictated by the exhaust vents of the Divinities. If a Seraph overheats, the world below burns. If they enter cooling mode, the world freezes.

## Starting Scenarios

### A) The Classic - "The Static-Born"

[Awakening in the slums of a Cathedral City]

**Starting Kit:** A "Receiver" (a crude radio-headset), tattered robes, and a rusty 5-meter "Acolyte" Frame (worker bot).
**Initial Challenges:** The noise. The air is filled with overwhelming static (God-Voice). You must learn to "tune out" the divine frequencies or go mad.
**First Contact:** A street-preacher who claims your specific brain-wave frequency is a "Chosen Channel."

### B) The Academy - "The Signal Corps"

[Training as a communications officer/priest]

**Starting Kit:** High-fidelity Transceiver, priestly vestments, access to a 15-meter "Paladin" Frame.
**Initial State:** Novice Monk.
**Academy Features:** Learning to encrypt prayers, deciphering the "Will of the Gear," combat training against "Heretic" signals.
**Social Dynamics:** Factions arguing over which Celestial Mecha is the "True God."

### C) The Guild - "The Scale-Miners"

[Scavenging the debris of divine battles]

**Starting Kit:** Anti-Radiation suit, laser-cutter, gravity-clamp boots.
**Guild Options:**

* *The Scalers:* They climb the corpses of fallen Gods (mountains of metal) to mine "Divine Alloy."
* *The Spark-Hunters:* They try to bottle leaking coolant or energy.

**Initial Reputation:** F-Rank Scavenger.
**First Missions:** Climb the "Thumb of Malak" (a 500km fallen debris field) to extract a sensor node before the auto-turrets wake up.

### D) The Tournament - "The Aspirant"

[You arrive precisely as the Ascension Protocol begins]

**Starting Kit:** You are summoned directly into the arena waiting room with a customized, soul-bound Mecha (C-Rank).
**Party Composition:** You are solo, but surrounded by millions of other contestants.
**Initial Mission:** Survive the "Qualifiers"—a battle royale of 10,000 mechs. Only 100 survive.

### E) The Deep End - "The Bug in the System"

[You spawn *inside* a Divinity]

**Starting Kit:** Nothing.
**Immediate Threats:** You are microscopic parasites inside a 7,000km tall machine. You must survive the automated immune system (drones the size of skyscrapers) and escape before you are "sanitized."
**Survival Rewards:** If you escape, you steal a "Divine Code Fragment" (A cheat skill that lets you command lower machines).

### F) The Transplant - " The Prophet"

[Summoned by a Divinity that has gone silent]

**Starting Kit:** A direct neural link to a Dead God.
**Recruiter:** **Omega-Prime** (A deceased Seraph-Class).
**Purpose:** You are its Avatar. You must pilot its corpse (or a fragment of it) to avenge its death.

### G) The Regression - "The Failed God"

[You won the tournament once, reached the Forge, failed the crafting, and died. Now you are back.]

**Regression Path:** Reincarnation.
**Starting Kit:** Knowledge of the Forge's location and the "Recipes" for Divinity.
**Impending Disaster:** The "Silence of the Spheres"—a cosmic event where all Gods shut down, leaving the world defenseless against the Void.

## Geography & Major Locations

### The Shell (Mortal Realm)

* **Vatican-01:** The capital city, essentially a massive radio dish aimed at the Heavens.
* **The Scrapyard of Angels:** A continent-sized region where a single Seraph fell 1,000 years ago. People live in cities built inside its ribcage.

### The Heavens (Orbital Layer)

* **The War-Front:** The orbital belt where the Divinities fight the "Void-Demons" (Anti-Matter Mecha).

### The Destination

* **Omnissiah's Anvil (The Forge World):** A separate dimension accessible only to Champions. A planet made entirely of shifting gears and furnaces, ruled by **The Primesmith**.

## Factions & Politics

### Major Factions

**The Orthodoxy of the Gear (Lawful Neutral):**

* *Philosophy:* Worship the Broadcasts. Do not question the Gods.
* *Methods:* Mass prayer to boost the signal strength of their patron Seraphs.
* *Leadership:* The Grand Lector (A cyborg Pope).

**The Static-Noise (Chaotic Evil):**

* *Philosophy:* Disrupt the frequencies. Jam the Gods. Let chaos reign.
* *Methods:* Terrorist attacks on radio towers, summoning "Glitch-Demons."
* *Leadership:* **Signal-Lost** (An AI virus).

**The Ascendants (True Neutral):**

* *Philosophy:* Gods are made, not born. We will join them.
* *Methods:* Participation in the Tournament. Hoarding materials.

## Power Systems & Magic

### Frequency & Resonance

**Primary System:** **Broadcast Theology.**

* **Prayer is Power:** The Gods run on faith, which is converted into energy via "Soul-Frequencies."
* **The Noise:** Mortals can tune into these channels. Listening to a War-God's frequency grants **[Strength Buffs]**. Listening to a Mercy-God grants **[Healing]**.
* **Risk:** Tuning into a frequency that is too powerful will fry your brain (Head explosion).

**Secondary System:** **The Ascension.**

* To become a God, you don't just level up. You must physically replace your body with Divine Alloy, piece by piece, until nothing organic remains, yet your Soul (The Ghost) stays intact.

## The Tournament of Champions: "The Ascension Protocol"

Every four years (Earth time), the **Golden Gate** opens.
**Entry Requirement:** A soul from another world (Isekai Protagonist) or a native Rank-A Pilot.

**The Prize:** Teleportation to **Omnissiah's Anvil**.

**The Quest of the Forge:**
Upon arrival, **The Primesmith** (a 500-meter tall, stoic, hammer-wielding entity) gives you a blueprint. To forge your God-Body, you must acquire impossible materials. There is no time limit. You stop when you die.

**The Materials List (Examples):**

1. **The Heart of a Dying Star:** You must physically enter a sun and extract the core.
2. **The Silence Between Thoughts:** A metaphysical material found only in the void.
3. **The Tear of a Pacifist:** You must convince a War-God to feel regret.

**Success Rate:** 0.000001%. Most aspirants end up as statues in the Primesmith's garden.

## Isekai Integration Elements

### Modern Knowledge Applications

* **Encryption:** The natives pray on open channels. A modern programmer introducing **End-to-End Encryption** essentially creates "Unstoppable Magic" that the Gods cannot intercept or block.
* **Signal Triangulation:** Pinpointing a God's exact location for a strike.

### Cheat Skill Synergies

* **[Infinite Inventory]:** Essential for the Forge Quest. How else do you carry a Star Core?
* **[Universal Translation]:** Allows you to understand the "Static" of the Gods instantly, bypassing years of training.

## Signature Quests

### Repeatable Activities

* **Tower Defense:** Protecting a Broadcast Spire from waves of Void-Demons.
* **Signal Boosting:** Climbing high peaks to install repeaters for the Orthodoxy.

### Major Quest Lines

**"The Metatron Protocol"**
You discover a frequency that controls the "Voice of God."

* *Stakes:* If you broadcast on this channel, you can command *all* Seraphs at once.

**"The God-Eater"**
A massive biological entity is eating the Dyson Sphere from the outside. The Gods are too busy fighting each other to notice. You must unite them.

## Unique Deus-Ex-Machina Features

### The Prayer-Stream

* **Feature:** Players can set up their own "Frequency." If they perform heroic deeds, NPCs will start tuning in. This generates **Faith Points (FP)**.
* **Usage:** FP can be used to fuel powerful abilities or, eventually, to begin the construction of your own Divine Chassis.

### Visual Scale

* **Narrative Rule:** The GM must constantly reinforce the scale. A "mountain" in the distance stands up and walks away. The "moon" blinks. The player feels small, making their rise to power even more epic.

## Achievement System

### Broadcast Honors

* **"Clear Signal":** Maintain a 100% Sync Rate during a solar flare.
* **"Heretic":** Survive being excommunicated by the Orthodoxy (and the subsequent orbital bombardment).

### Ultimate Achievements

* **"Deus Est Machina":** Complete the Primesmith's Quest, forge your body, and ascend as a new Star in the Heavens.
* **"The Breaker of Chains":** Destroy the Forge and free the mortals from the cycle of worship.
