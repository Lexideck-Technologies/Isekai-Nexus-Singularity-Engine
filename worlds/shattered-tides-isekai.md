# ISEKAI WORLD: THE SHATTERED TIDES

## World Classification // (Revised for v4.2)

**World Type:** [S Rank] - [High-Concept / Sky-Ocean Survival / Gravity-Fantasy]
**Danger Level:** [Extreme] - Environmental hazards are constant. Miscalculating a jump or running out of air results in death by asphyxiation or falling into the endless void.
**Power Ceiling:** [Planetary/Physics Manipulation] - Mastery over gravity wells and the ability to stabilize entire continents.

## World Overview

The Shattered Tides is a world broken apart. Eons ago, the planet exploded, leaving behind a nebula of breathable Aether-Mist and massive chunks of terrain floating in a zero-gravity expanse. There is no "sea level." There is only the Drift. Civilization clings to "Islands"—floating asteroids stabilized by ancient, humming Keystones that generate localized gravity. Between these islands lies the Void, a sea of clouds where ships rigged with solar sails and Aether-turbines hunt for resources.

Life here is defined by verticality and vertigo. The physics are chaotic; "Tide Storms" sweep through the nebula, randomly inverting gravity vectors. A ship sailing smoothly can suddenly find itself "falling" upward into the freezing stratosphere. In this world, gold is worthless. The true currencies are **Atmosphere** (compressed breathable air) and **Keystones** (gravity anchors). Without air, you suffocate; without a Keystone, you drift forever.

As a **Void-Mariner**, you enter a life of high-stakes harpoon hunting and daring gliding. You are one of the few brave or desperate enough to navigate the chaos, hunting the colossal bio-luminescent Sky-Krakens that swim through the mist, seeking the mythical "Solid Ground"—the legendary core of the world rumored to exist at the center of the perpetual storm known as the Maelstrom.

**Climate/Environment:** Perpetual twilight illuminated by distant, shattered moon shards. Temperature fluctuates with altitude. The greatest threat is "The Thinning"—pockets of vacuum where air pressure drops instantly.

## Geography & Major Locations

### The Mid-Drift (Civilized Zone)

* **Port Aero:** A massive city built on a cluster of chained-together asteroids. It is the trade hub of the sector, smelling of ozone and burning oil.
* **The Rust Reefs:** A graveyard of ancient starships from before the world shattered. Scavengers pick through the hulls for technology.

### The Deep Strata (High Pressure)

* **The Fog-Floor:** A dense layer of heavy, toxic gas. Visibility is zero. Giant, blind leviathans dwell here.
* **Sanctum of Silence:** A monastery hidden in the deep fog where monks train to hold their breath for hours.

### The Zenith (Upper Atmosphere)

* **The Crystal Peaks:** Islands of pure ice and crystal. The air is too thin to breathe without heavy gear. Home to the wealthiest elites who can afford pumped air.

## Factions & Politics

### Major Factions

**The Aer-Barons (Lawful Evil):**

* *Philosophy:* Air is a privilege, not a right. Profit governs survival.
* *Methods:* Monopolizing atmosphere scrubbers; indentured servitude for air debt.
* *Leadership:* Baroness Kaelia, the "Iron Lung."
* *Reputation Effects:* Hated by the poor, respected by merchants.

**The Anchor-Watch (Lawful Neutral):**

* *Philosophy:* The Keystones are sacred. Gravity must be maintained at all costs.
* *Methods:* Militaristic defense of islands; hunting "Gravity Thieves."
* *Leadership:* High Warden Thaddeus.
* *Reputation Effects:* Grants access to restricted stable zones.

**The Free Drift (Chaotic Good):**

* *Philosophy:* The sky belongs to everyone. No borders, no air taxes.
* *Methods:* Piracy against Barons, smuggling air to the poor.
* *Leadership:* Captain "Red-Wind."

## Power Systems & Magic

### Graviturgy (Vector Arts)

Magic in this world is the manipulation of weight and direction.

**Casting Method:** Gestural (using hands to "pull" or "push" fabric of space) and Focus-based (using Keystone shards).
**Mana Source:** **Core-Resonance**. Using magic strains the user's internal equilibrium (causing nausea/vertigo).
**Skill Requirements:** High Intelligence (Calculation) and Constitution (G-Force resistance).

### Aether-Tech

Steam-punk technology powered by compressed Aether-Mist.

* **Examples:** Pneumatic grappling hooks, gliders with jet-assist, pressure-plate armor.

## Isekai Integration Elements

### Modern Knowledge Applications

* **Aerodynamics:** Designing better gliders/sails (Airfoils vs flat canvas) gives a massive speed advantage.
* **Scuba Physics:** Understanding decompression sickness (The Bends) allows the player to survive rapid ascents that kill natives.
* **Meteorology:** Predicting pressure fronts allows the player to navigate storms rather than hide from them.

### Cheat Skill Synergies

* **[Featherfall]:** Absolute immunity to the world's greatest danger (falling).
* **[Inventory Box]:** Infinite storage for Air Canisters creates an infinite wealth loop (smuggling air without weight).
* **[Analysis]:** Can see the "durability" of gravity vectors.

### Romance & Relationship Opportunities

Relationships in The Shattered Tides are intense and fast-paced, born of "foxhole" mentality.

* **The Mechanic:** Bonding over fixing the ship while oxygen runs low.
* **The Rival Captain:** A enemies-to-lovers trope involving racing through asteroid fields.

## Signature Quests

### Repeatable Activities

* **Air-Harvesting:** Flying into dense nebula pockets to fill tanks. (Reward: Currency/Survival)
* **Salvage Runs:** Diving into the Deep Strata to recover tech from fallen ships. (Reward: Rare parts)

### Major Quest Lines

**"The Anchor of the World":**

The search for the primary Keystone that holds the shattered planet together.

* *Stakes:* The current Keystones are failing. If the Anchor isn't found, the islands will drift apart into the frozen dark.

**"The Breath Rebellion":**

Overthrowing the Aer-Barons' monopoly on oxygen.

* *Rewards:* Free air for the sector, title of "Liberator," massive bounty on your head.

## Unique [The Shattered Tides] Features

### Gravity Mechanics

* **Local vs. Universal:** Gravity is generated by objects (Keystones), not the planet. Walking away from a Keystone means entering Zero-G.
* **The Flip:** During storms, "Down" becomes "Up." Players must secure themselves or fall into the sky.

### The Breath Economy

* **Air is Life:** Your UI has a constant Oxygen Meter. Actions deplete it. Money buys time.
* **Keystone Decay:** Islands are slowly losing gravity. Land value is determined by the "Hum" of its Keystone.

## Achievement System

### Exploration

* **[Void-Walker]:** Survive 10 minutes in the vacuum without a suit (requires high stats).
* **[Deep Diver]:** Reach the bottom of the Fog-Floor and return.

* **[Kraken-Slayer]:** Deliver the killing blow to a Sky-Kraken.
* **[Ace Pilot]:** Win a ship-to-ship battle without taking hull damage.

### Ultimate Achievements

* **[Terra Firma]:** Discover "Solid Ground" and plant a flag on the core of the world.
